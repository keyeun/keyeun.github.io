Underworld of Seoul


(Link)Worklog
2021
Icon
Design
School Work

Seoul's subway system is widely regarded as one of the best in the world. I prefer to take subway than any other public transportation because each line has their own characteristic. Sometimes, the seoul metropolitan subway system seems to support a busy life of Seoul in the underworld. Therefore, I created a poster named 'Underworld of Seoul', containing 12 variations of visual lists of seoul metro lines.
