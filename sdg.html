Carbon Cast a Shadow on Earth


(Link)Worklog
2021
Infographics
Design
School Work

The poster started with the idea that carbon dioxide is like a shadow on the earth. I produced the infographic in the hope that South Korea will achieve its 2030 reduction goal to drive out the shadows on the earth and regain the light. I used the big area chart in the background, with past on the left and the future on the right. Also, it is mainly based on Korean climate crisis data.
