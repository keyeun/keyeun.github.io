2025 Garbage Crisis

It is a infographic poster about a conflict over Sudokwon Landfill Site(SLS) in South Korea. Sudokwon Landfill in Incheon has been taking waste from the metropolitan area(Seoul, Gyeonggi Province, Incheon) since 1992. However, it can be used untill 2025, when the contract for the current landfill ends. This infographics points out that 44 districts, 75% of total districts, violated dumping cap which deals with household waste. Moreover, it criticizes that significant amounts of garbage has been brought in SLS for more than 10 years.
