Poliscore is an interactive opinion poll aggregator website made with with Team Underscore and Seoul Broadcasting System. It was produced for 2022 South Korean presidential election and local election. Poliscore displays a single metric generated based on the collected poll results. It also shows the number of comments, nested reply ratio, and profanity of comments written by each portal's users by political orientation.
