This is a quantified-self project based upon instagram stories. Since 2018, the year I entered the university, I have used my instagram as a diary. Especially, I uploaded stories more than posts because they expired after 24 hours. From 2018 to 2021, I uploaded over 2000 stories. I thought these data could be a good ingredient for my data visualization project. After downloading stories from instagram, I labelled mood status(happy, gloomy, neutral) and category of each instagram story. Then I analyzed them by year and month. Read 'How to read' first and enjoy the poster!
